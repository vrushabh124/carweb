Computer Vision Model

Computer Vision (CV) is a field of artificial intelligence that trains computers to interpret and understand the visual world. In autonomous cars, CV models are paramount for processing data from onboard cameras, allowing the vehicle to perceive its environment in real-time. This perception is crucial for everything from basic navigation to complex decision-making in dynamic traffic situations.

Sensor Fusion Models

It's the intelligent process of combining data from multiple, diverse sensors to create a more complete, accurate, and robust understanding of the vehicle's surroundings. By leveraging the complementary nature of different sensor technologies, autonomous cars can overcome the limitations of individual sensors, ensuring higher levels of safety and reliability in all driving conditions.
